# Privacy Policy

**Echos of Mind**
Last Updated: 02/27/2026

## 1. Overview

Echos of Mind is a private self-awareness and journaling application.

We are built around a simple principle:
Your emotional patterns belong to you.

This Privacy Policy explains:

* What we collect
* Why we collect it
* How it is processed
* What we do not do with your data

We do not sell data.
We do not run ads.
We do not build cross-user behavioral profiles.

Trust is structural to this product.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Echos of Mind, you may provide:

* Journal entries
* Mood check-ins
* Optional voice recordings
* Account information (email, name if provided)

You control what you write.

We do not require unnecessary personal details.

---

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

To operate the service securely, we may collect:

* Device type
* App version
* Basic technical logs (for debugging and stability)
* Authentication metadata

This data is used strictly for performance, security, and service reliability.

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to:

* Detect personal baseline patterns
* Identify sustained deviation over time
* Generate reflective prompts
* Improve system stability and accuracy

All insights are derived from your historical data only.

There is:

* No comparison with other users
* No population ranking
* No behavioral advertising
* No engagement manipulation

We do not diagnose.
We do not label.
We reflect change.

---

## 4. Voice and Text Processing

### Voice

Voice recordings remain processed locally on your device whenever possible.

### Text Analysis

Text entries may be processed through secure APIs to extract emotional signals and pattern indicators.

This processing is:

* Automated
* Used only for personal insight generation
* Not used for profiling
* Not shared with third parties for advertising

We do not manually read your journal entries.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard encryption practices to protect your data:

* Encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
* Secure server infrastructure
* Access controls
* Role-based internal restrictions

We are structured to minimize internal access to user content.

Where possible, sensitive content is encrypted at rest.

---

## 6. What We Explicitly Do Not Do

We do not:

* Sell your data
* Show ads
* Share your emotional patterns with third parties
* Build cross-user predictive behavioral models
* Use your journal content for marketing
* Monetize through surveillance

Your data supports only your continuity.

---

## 7. Data Retention

You control your data.

You may:

* Delete journal entries
* Delete your account
* Request permanent data removal

Upon deletion, your data will be permanently erased from our active systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where legally required to retain minimal security logs.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted infrastructure providers for:

* Authentication
* Cloud hosting
* Text analysis APIs

These providers process data strictly under service agreements and are not permitted to use your information for independent purposes.

We do not authorize third-party marketing access to your data.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

Echos of Mind is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum digital consent age in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect data from children without verified parental consent.
